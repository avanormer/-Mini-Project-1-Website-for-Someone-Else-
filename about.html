Our Story

The Service Benefit (TSB) is a dedicated platform that amplifies the voices and impacts of non-profit organizations worldwide. Beyond social media buzz, we exist to bridge the gap between passionate volunteers and meaningful causes, fostering sustainable change through authentic storytelling and community engagement.

Meet the Director

Deniz Ketenci is the visionary founder and director of The Service Benefit. With a background in social entrepreneurship and a passion for global impact, Deniz leads our mission to connect people with opportunities to make a real difference in the world.

How We Work

Our process is simple yet impactful: We feature outstanding NGOs, create compelling mini-commercials and storytelling content that highlights their work, and share these stories across our platforms to inspire action and promote donations and volunteer opportunities.
